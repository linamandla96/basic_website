Historical Places

District six Museum

District six museum is a museum in the former inner city residental area. It was founded in 1986 and the museum in 1994. It is the memorial to the forced movement of 60.00 people.

Cape town City Hall

City hall was designed as the result of a public competition. The tower of the city hall has a turret clock. On February 1900 Nelson Mandela made his first public speech from the balcony of the city hall.

Castle of GoodHope

Castle of goodhope was built by the Dutch East India company between 1666 and 1679. The castle was never attacted. In 1936 the Castle of goodhope was declared a national monument.
